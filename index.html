About Ctrl+Decode

Welcome to Ctrl+Decode! We're an exciting program dedicated to teaching kids how to code in the world of Artificial Intelligence (AI) while exploring the important concepts of its ethics and their responsibility.

Our goal is to empower the next generation of coders to create smarter, safer, and more thoughtful technology. From basic coding principles to fun AI projects, Ctrl+Code is the perfect starting point for young minds who want to shape the future of tech!

The program will always culminate in a final project where kids will use anything from basic algorithms to advanced machine learning concepts to create a solution to a problem they're passionate about! This will fully depend on a student's comfort level with programming by the end of the sessions, and is dependent on their comfort level!
